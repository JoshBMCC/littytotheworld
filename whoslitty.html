Who is Litty?

Litty is a being from the planet Beautiful who cares a great deal about vibrating at high frequency and in order to constantly do so, everyone around him has to have a good attitude and give off good energy. On his home planet the name Litty means joy. When the leader of the planet picked up a great amount of bad energy coming from a sister planet, Earth, Litty was sent on a mission to resotre the balance of the universe by changing all the bad energy on Earth to good energy! Little does he know he has his work cut out for him...
